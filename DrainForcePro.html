Common Issues

Drainage Problems
We Solve Every Day

Ignoring drainage issues leads to bigger, more expensive problems. Here's what we handle — quickly and professionally.

🚿

Blocked Kitchen & Bathroom Drains

Grease, food waste, hair, soap scum — the most common cause of slow or completely blocked household drains. We clear them fast and identify if a deeper structural issue is the root cause.

🌊

Overflowing Gullies & External Drains

Overflowing external drains and gullies can flood gardens, driveways, and even enter properties. We respond rapidly to prevent property damage and health hazards from sewage overflow.

🔁

Recurring Blockages

If you're unblocking the same drain repeatedly, there's an underlying problem. Our CCTV surveys identify hidden defects — collapsed pipes, root intrusion, or incorrect gradients — causing repeat issues.

🚨

Sewage Flooding & Backups

Sewage backup is a serious health hazard requiring immediate action. We provide emergency same-day response for drain flooding, sewage backups, and any situation where water is entering your property.

🌿

Root Intrusion in Pipes

Tree and shrub roots are a silent drain destroyer — seeking out moisture through hairline cracks and gradually collapsing pipework. Our root cutting and relining service permanently resolves this.

💨

Bad Smells from Drains

Foul odours from drains indicate bacteria buildup, partial blockages, or damaged drain seals. We identify the exact cause and eliminate the source — not just mask the smell.
